Early Access

We'd love to learn what your team needs. Get in touch at support@krypt.co and we'll help you get started.

  • Kryptonite Core

    All the features of Kryptonite Core.

  • Policies

    Invite your team members to join your Kryptonite Command team. Create custom temporary approval rules and policies.

  • Real-time Audit Logs

    Automatically export and aggregate your team's SSH access logs in real-time. Store logs in an AWS S3 bucket or forward logs to a third-party service.

  • Trustless Infrastructure

    Command is built on top of an end-to-end verifiable infrastructure powered by block chain technology. We host the block chain, but only your team is able to write to it. You can trust that public keys, logs, and policies are authentic and unmodified by krypt.co or any other party.

  • Real-time Host Pinning

    Protect your team from man-in-the-middle (MITM) attacks. Pin public keys of known servers and securely and seamlessly distribute them to your team members in real-time. Host verification is performed in the Kryptonite app so your team members will verifiably know where they're connecting to.

  • Public-key Management

    The one place to manage your team's SSH and PGP public-keys. Easily onboard new team members by granting access to the resources they need to do their work. When a developer leaves your team, easily ensure their access is revoked in real-time.

  • Two-Person Rule

    Require approval from 2-out-of-3 (or more) team members for pushing production code or logging into sensitive servers. Satisfy PCI Dual Control compliance by ensuring high-stakes resources are not accessible by any single team member.

    Need help? We'll provide support by phone, chat, or email. Contact us support@krypt.co to get started.
